Providing Support and Assistance for Refugees

REFUGEE SUPPORT SERVICES

Service ID Service Type Service Description Service Details
001 Medical Services Access to healthcare facilities, free check-ups, and medical assistance. Available at partnered clinics and hospitals.
002 Legal Services Assistance with immigration paperwork, asylum applications, and legal counseling. Pro bono legal aid provided by volunteer lawyers.
003 ESL Classes English as a Second Language classes for refugees. Available online and in community centers.
004 Grocery Deliveries Weekly grocery deliveries for families in need. Request-based delivery system operated by volunteers.
005 Carpooling Transportation assistance for medical visits, job interviews, and essential trips. Volunteer drivers provide scheduled pickups.
006 Items Needed List/Delivery or Pickup Essential supplies such as clothing, furniture, and hygiene products. Donors contribute, and items are delivered or picked up.
007 Emergency Services Immediate assistance in case of emergencies, including shelter and crisis aid. 24/7 helpline available for urgent situations.
